OFAC has stated that digital currency address listings on the SDN list are "not likely to be exhaustive" (see OFAC FAQ 562). Sanctioned parties frequently generate new addresses. The absence of an address from this tool's output does NOT mean it is not associated with a sanctioned person or entity.

This tool is NOT a substitute for a comprehensive sanctions compliance program. Users are responsible for ensuring their own compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including OFAC sanctions.

The data is derived from publicly available OFAC publications. The tool's chain-level categorization involves heuristic address format matching that may contain errors, particularly for multi-chain tokens on EVM-compatible networks. Always verify against the authoritative OFAC source data.
